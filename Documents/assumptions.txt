------------------------------------------------------------
ATMOSPHERE (ISA_calculator.py)
------------------------------------------------------------
- ISA sea-level temperature  : T0  = 288.15 K
- ISA sea-level pressure     : p0  = 101 325 Pa
- ISA sea-level density      : rho0 = 1.225 kg/m³
- Tropospheric lapse rate    : L   = 0.0065 K/m
- Tropopause altitude        : h   = 11 000 m
- Specific gas constant air  : R   = 287.05 J/(kg·K)
- Ratio of specific heats    : gamma = 1.4
- Stratosphere is treated as fully isothermal above 11 000 m

MISSION ANALYSIS (mission.py)
------------------------------------------------------------
Breguet fractions:
- Taxi / warm-up fraction     : w1/w0 = 0.98  (fixed)
- Climb fraction formula      : w2/w1 = 1.0065 − 0.0325 × M_cruise
- Landing / approach fraction : w7/w6 = 0.99  (fixed)
- Post-landing taxi fraction  : w8/w7 = 0.992 (fixed)
- Trapped-fuel allowance      : +1 % added to total fuel weight
- Reserve time default        : 0.5 hr

Aerodynamic defaults:
- Default Oswald efficiency   : e     = 0.80
- Default propeller efficiency: eta   = 0.80  (cruise, global)
- Default SFC                 : 0.5 1/hr

CL / CD0 values by engine type (used in W/P–W/S diagram):
                         Clean   Take-off   Landing   CD0
  Jet                   : 1.2     1.5        1.7      0.015
  Turboprop             : 1.4     1.7        2.0      0.020
  Piston                : 1.3     1.5        1.8      0.020

Raymer empty-weight fraction regression (Table 3.1 coefficients + UAV caps):
  Jet       : We/W0 = 1.67  × W0_lbs^-0.16,  capped at 0.50
  Turboprop : We/W0 = 2.75  × W0_lbs^-0.18,  capped at 0.52
  Piston    : We/W0 = 0.97  × W0_lbs^-0.06,  capped at 0.58

Sizing bounds:
- MTOW hard upper limit: 100 000 kg (outside UAV scope)
- Bisection convergence tolerance: (hi − lo)/max(hi,1) < 1e-4

ENGINE TYPE SELECTION (Drone.py / Engine.py)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Mach threshold for jet selection (altitude-graduated):
    h <= 9 000 m  : M >= 0.40 → Jet
    h <= 15 000 m : M >= 0.50 → Jet
    h >  15 000 m : M >= 0.60 → Jet
- Below Mach threshold:
    Mission objective == "High Endurance" → Turboprop
    Altitude > 4 500 m                   → Turboprop
    Otherwise                            → Piston
- Piston engine practical ceiling        : 4 500 m
- Turboprop practical ceiling            : 9 000 m

Engine count from MTOW (Engine.py):
    MTOW <  2 700 kg  → 1 engine
    MTOW < 30 000 kg  → 2 engines
    MTOW >= 30 000 kg → 4 engines  (safety-net only; outside UAV scope)


------------------------------------------------------------
JET ENGINE (JetEngine.py)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Thrust lapse model         : T_alt/T_sl = (rho_alt/rho_sl)^0.75
- Bypass ratio formula       : BPR = 15 × (1 − T/W)², clamped to [1, 12]
- Fan radius                 : 90 % of nacelle radius
- Core radius                : 40 % of fan radius
- Fan disc longitudinal pos  : 15 % of nacelle length from inlet
- Nozzle taper start         : 80 % of nacelle length
- Nacelle inlet taper profile : 0–15 % of length (linear in/out)
- Inlet radius ratio default : 0.85
- Nozzle radius ratio default: 0.70


------------------------------------------------------------
PROPELLER ENGINE (PropellerEngine.py)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Propeller efficiency (cruise) : eta = 0.82
- Propeller efficiency (climb)  : eta = 0.75
- Rate-of-climb design target   : ROC = 3.0 m/s
- Altitude diameter scaling     : D_alt = D_sl × sqrt(rho_sl / rho_alt)
  (actuator-disk: thrust area ∝ 1/rho → diameter ∝ 1/sqrt(rho))
- Blade length cap              : min(15 % semi-span, 1.5 × fuselage radius)
  (nose-mount: fuselage-radius cap; wing-mount: semi-span cap)
- Spinner radius                : 15 % of propeller diameter
- Spinner length                : 1.5 × spinner radius
- Nacelle radius (prop)         : equals spinner radius
- Nacelle length (prop)         : 3.5 × nacelle radius
- Blade root chord formula      : 0.065 × D_prop × (n_blades / 2)
- Blade tip chord               : 30 % of blade root chord
- n_blades from solidity        : target_solidity × π × R / c_root,
  rounded and clamped to [2, 6]
- Solidity bump for V > 80 m/s  : target_solidity × 1.4, capped at 0.30


------------------------------------------------------------
ENGINE POSITIONING (Engine.py)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Wing-mount spanwise station   : 35 % semi-span
- Prop x-offset (forward)       : blade_length × tan(sweep_LE) + 0.05 m
- Jet x-offset (forward)        : −35 % nacelle length ahead of wing LE
- Jet z-offset (downward)       : −2 × nacelle radius below wing LE
- Single jet placement          : dorsal-rear at 85 % fuselage length,
                                  Z on top of fuselage (Z = fuselage radius)
- Single prop placement         : nose tractor at fuselage origin (x = 0)


------------------------------------------------------------
WING ASPECT RATIO (Drone.py)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Jet     : AR = 4.737 × M_cruise^-0.979, clamped to [6.0, 12.0]
- Turboprop: AR = 9.2  (statistical MALE default)
- Piston  : AR = 7.6  (statistical GA default)
- Wing root chord is capped at 40 % of estimated fuselage length;
  if exceeded, AR is increased to stay within that chord limit
- Wing max semi-span capped at 4 × estimated fuselage length


------------------------------------------------------------
UAV CLASS CLASSIFICATION (Drone.py / PayloadRules)
------------------------------------------------------------
By range:
    < 150 km  → small
    150–500 km → medium
    > 500 km  → large
By endurance:
    < 4 hr   → small
    4–10 hr  → medium
    > 10 hr  → large
(Most restrictive class is used when range and endurance disagree)

Load factor baseline by class:
    small  → 4.0 g
    medium → 3.5 g
    large  → 2.5 g
Adjustments (additive):
    Mission objective = "High Speed"    → +1.0 g
    Mission objective = "High Endurance"→ −0.5 g
    Payload includes weapon             → +1.0 g
    Payload includes radar              → +0.5 g

Mach limits:
    Maximum Mach = 1.5 × cruise Mach   (for V-n diagram sizing)
    Loiter speed seed = 70 % of cruise speed

FUSELAGE GEOMETRY (Fuselage.py)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Fineness ratio used for Roskam fallback: l/d = 14
  (i.e., radius = length / 28 when no payload or fuel tank is defined)
- Structural minimum radius: max(0.03 × length, 0.06 m)
- Structural CG assumed at 45 % of fuselage length
- Nose-cone and tailcone use an elliptical cross-section blend profile
- Gap between payload bay and fuel tank: 50 mm (hard-coded)
- Default nosecone end      : 10 % of fuselage length
- Default tailcone start    : 70 % of fuselage length
- Default payload nose clearance: 0.20 m

UNDERCARRIAGE (Undercarriage.py)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Gear configuration           : Tricycle (fixed-wing UAV standard)
- n_main_struts                : 2 for MTOW ≤ 50 000 kg; 4 above
- Wheels per strut             : 1 for MTOW ≤ 5 700 kg; 2 above
- Tyre diameter formula (UAV-modified): d = 0.034 × MTOW^0.21
  (Roskam Tables extrapolate poorly below ~500 kg; exponent reduced)
- Tyre width                   : 35 % of tyre outer diameter
- Strut radius                 : 25 % of wheel major radius
- Axle radius                  : 60 % of strut radius
- Nose gear longitudinal pos   : 10 % of fuselage length
- Main gear longitudinal pos   : 60 % of fuselage length
- Prop/nacelle ground clearance margin: 0.18 m
- Tailstrike design rotation angle: 12°
- Tip-over criterion angle     : 35° from vertical
- Minimum lateral track        : 3.5 × wheel major radius
- Axle half-width (dual wheel) : 1.2 × wheel major radius

------------------------------------------------------------

- Tail aerodynamic efficiency: eta_h = 0.90
- Target static margin range: 5 – 15 % MAC
  (FBW aircraft may accept down to −10 %)
- Engine CG assumed at wing aerodynamic centre
- Fuel CG assumed at wing aerodynamic centre
- Fuel tank structure CG assumed at wing aerodynamic centre

------------------------------------------------------------

  (used when engine_mass is not supplied)

  (composite integral tank estimate; Roskam range 2–3 %)


------------------------------------------------------------
FUEL TANK (FuelTank.py)
------------------------------------------------------------
- Ullage fraction          : 5 % of net fuel volume
- Tank structure fraction  : 5 % of net fuel volume
- Volume sizing factor     : 1 / (1 − 0.05 − 0.05) ≈ 1.111
- Default tank aspect ratio: AR = 3.0  (length / diameter)
- Minimum AR enforced      : 1.01  (prevents zero cylinder length)
- Fuselage radius clearance: tank outer radius × 1.03  (+3 %)
- Tank geometry            : capsule (cylinder + two hemispherical caps)
- Minimum circle radius in loft: max(R × 0.002, 1e-4 m)  (degenerate-profile guard)
